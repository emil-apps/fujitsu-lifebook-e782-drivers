Anytime USB Charge Utility Help

 

Overview

 

You can change the settings of "Anytime USB Charge" which enables you to charge USB peripherals without PC power on.

 

Instructions

 

Change "Anytime USB Charge" settings

 

1. Power on PC and logon as an Administrative user or one of the member of Administrators group.

 

2. Press [Start] button and select [All Programs]->[Anytime USB Charge Utility]->[Setting]. "Anytime USB Charge Utility" window appears.

- When you see "User Account Control" Window, please click [Yes] or [Continue].

- The above window is one of the examples which might be displayed. The window might be different according to the environment.

 

3. Click a radio button   to select an item you prefer. The button will be turned to   and press [Change] button to apply the changes.

 

4. Press [OK] button in a window which tells you the updates have been done.
